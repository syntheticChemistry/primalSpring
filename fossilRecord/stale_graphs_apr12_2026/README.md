# Archived Graphs — April 12, 2026

Graphs archived during the seasonal tightening of primalSpring's intermediary role.

These are **not deleted** — they're fossil record. They may be revived when
springs evolve further and compositions mature.

## What was archived

- **`science/`** (13 graphs): Speculative domain compositions (gaming mesh, paper lifecycle,
  neuromorphic, reproducibility audit). These belong to individual springs as they evolve
  their compositions, not to primalSpring's core atomic validation role.

- **`sketches/`** (12 graphs across 4 subdirs): Exploration sketches for bonding patterns,
  mixed atomics, and validation patterns. Forward-looking but not exercised by any experiment.

- **`gen4/`** (10 graphs): Garden-layer patterns (agentic, interactive, storytelling).
  These belong downstream in esotericWebb/gardens as they mature.
